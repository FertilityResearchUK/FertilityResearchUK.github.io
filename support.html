For Patients, Families & People Trying to Conceive

If you are experiencing fertility challenges, you are not alone. Many people face uncertainty, delays, or a lack of clear answers.

While Fertility Research UK is not a clinical service, we aim to support patients by:

  • Funding research that can improve diagnosis and treatment
  • Sharing evidence-based information
  • Amplifying patient priorities to researchers and decision-makers

Need medical advice?

Please speak to your GP or fertility specialist.

In urgent distress or crisis?

Contact NHS 111 (or 999 in an emergency).
