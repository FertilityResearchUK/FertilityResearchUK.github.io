bio: >
  Hira is a PhD student at the University of Edinburgh, working within the
  Adams group at the Institute of Genetics and Cancer and the Davies group at
  the Wellcome Centre for Cell Biology. Her research aims to deepen our
  understanding of how errors in meiosis, the process that produces eggs and
  sperm, can result in miscarriage and infertility. Before starting her PhD,
  Hira worked as a Clinical Research Practitioner at St Thomas' Hospital in
  London, supporting clinical trials in pregnancy complications. She completed
  her Master's in Reproductive and Developmental Biology at Imperial College
  London in 2022. Outside of research, she enjoys running and playing the
  piano.
quote_label: Why are you involved in FRUK?
quote: >
  I am passionate about addressing the rising infertility rates in the UK and
  believe that better-funded research is critically needed to overcome current
  barriers in the field and improve clinical outcomes for patients.
