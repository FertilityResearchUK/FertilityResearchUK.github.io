# ── Hero ──
hero_title: About Fertility Research UK
hero_tagline: >
  We believe research is one of the most effective ways to improve fertility
  outcomes, patient experience and long-term reproductive health.

# ── Commitments ──
commitments_intro: >
  Five principles that shape what we fund, who we listen to and how we work with
  researchers and communities across the UK.

commitments:
  - title: Evidence & impact
    description: Prioritising high-quality research with clear potential to improve understanding and care.
  - title: Lived experience
    description: Ensuring research priorities reflect what matters to patients and families.
  - title: Supporting research careers
    description: Growing the number of fertility researchers and supporting their careers by providing funding as well as a supportive network.
  - title: Collaboration
    description: Connecting clinicians, researchers, and communities to accelerate progress.
  - title: Equity
    description: Supporting work that addresses inequalities in access, outcomes and information.

# ── Vision & Mission ──
vision: >
  A UK where fertility challenges are better understood, better prevented and
  better treated—so everyone can access compassionate, evidence-based support
  and care.
mission: >
  To fund, support and champion fertility research across the UK and help
  translate findings into improved clinical practice, public understanding,
  and patient experience.

# ── Research Approach cards ──
research_approach:

    description: How funding decisions are made and what projects we support and why.
  - title: Progress & outcomes
    description: Regular updates on supported projects and their outcomes and impact.
  - title: Lived experience informs priorities
    description: How patient and public voices shape our research agenda and strategic direction.

# ── Integrity ──
integrity: >
  We aim to operate to high standards of governance and good practice. As we
  grow, we will publish our annual reports and financial statements, how
  research priorities are set and reviewed, the projects and people we
  support, and the impact achieved.
